===========================
Objectiv Bach Open Taxonomy
===========================

.. currentmodule:: bach_open_taxonomy

Objectiv Bach Open Taxonomy is an extension to the DataFrame and Series used in Bach. It allows for specific
functionality of data as stored by the Objectiv Tracker. This includes accessing methods specific to the
stacks global contexts and location stack, and also models from the open model hub.
